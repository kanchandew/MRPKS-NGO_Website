About Our Organization

Our History & Mission

Founded in 2000, Maa Renuka Pashu Kalyan Samiti has been at the forefront of animal welfare in our region. What began as a small initiative by Smt. Renuka Sonwane to feed stray dogs in her neighborhood has grown into a full-fledged organization with multiple rescue operations and a dedicated shelter.

Our mission is to create a compassionate society where every animal is treated with kindness and respect. We believe that even the smallest acts of kindness can make a world of difference in the lives of these voiceless creatures.

Smt. Renuka Sonwane

Secretary

Message from Our Secretary

"Every animal deserves love and care. At Maa Renuka Pashu Kalyan Samiti, we strive to be the voice for those who cannot speak for themselves. Our work is driven by compassion and the belief that kindness to animals makes us better humans."

- Smt. Renuka Sonwane, Secretary
